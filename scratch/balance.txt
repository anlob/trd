F: Fiat money
C: Cryptocurrency
P: Price
p: Percent factor

Fn = K * (Cn * Pn)

Pn = P0 * p^n
Fn = F0 * ((1+p)/2)^n
Cn = C0 * ((1+1/p)/2)^n


Proof: Fn = K * (Cn * Pn)

<=> (F0 * ((1+p)/2)^n) = K * (C0 * ((1+1/p)/2)^n) * (P0 * p^n)
<=> F0 = K * C0 * P0 * (((1+1/p)/2) * p / ((1+p)/2))^n

=>  (((1+1/p)/2) * p / ((1+p)/2))^n = 1
=>   ((1+1/p)/2) * p / ((1+p)/2) = 1
<=>  ((p+1)/2)       / ((1+p)/2) = 1
